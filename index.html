How to play

  1. Build your board. Pick 20 of the 30 characters. This is the board your opponent will study.
  2. Choose your secret. Pick 1 of your 20 to be your hidden character.
  3. Take turns. On your turn, ask a yes/no question about a trait, then either:
    • 🚫 Disable the characters that don't fit — but once you start disabling you can't guess this turn.
    • 🎯 Guess the opponent's secret. Right = you win. Wrong = you lose!
  4. Watch the counts. After each turn you can see how many cards your opponent has left open vs crossed out.
  5. Hit End Turn to pass play. First to correctly guess wins. 🏆
